About BYN Autoparts

20+ years of precision manufacturing in China's automotive parts hub — Yuhuan, Zhejiang

Our Story
Built in Yuhuan,
Trusted Worldwide

Taizhou Baoyingnuo Machinery Co., Ltd. was established in Yuhuan City, Zhejiang Province — the heart of China's automotive chassis parts manufacturing cluster. Over two decades, we have grown from a small forging workshop to a full-scale supplier with 1,000+ molds and a production team of hundreds.

Our focus has never shifted: precision chassis parts for global automotive aftermarkets. Every ball joint, tie rod end and stabilizer link that leaves our factory is built to meet or exceed OEM specifications.

Today we supply distributors, importers and tier-1 manufacturers across Europe, the Americas, Southeast Asia and the Middle East — backed by cross-references against MOOG, TRW, and original OEM part numbers.
