FAQ
Common Questions
What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

MOQ varies by product. For standard parts in our catalog, MOQ typically starts at 50–100 pieces per SKU. For custom or non-catalog parts, we'll confirm MOQ after reviewing your drawing or specifications. First-order MOQ is negotiable for new customers.

Can you supply parts compatible with MOOG or TRW reference numbers?

Yes. Our entire catalog is cross-referenced against MOOG and TRW numbers. Simply search by MOOG No. or TRW No. on our Products page, or include the reference number in your inquiry.

What is your standard lead time?

For in-stock or standard catalog items: 15–25 working days. For items requiring new tooling or custom specifications: 30–45 working days. Lead time is confirmed in the quotation.

What shipping terms do you offer?

We ship FOB Ningbo or FOB Shanghai. We can also arrange CIF, DDP or other Incoterms on request. Full export documentation (packing list, commercial invoice, certificate of origin) is provided with every shipment.

Do you offer private label / OEM packaging?

Yes. We support custom branded boxes, bags and labels. Minimum order for custom packaging is typically 200–500 pieces. Please include your packaging requirements in the inquiry form.
